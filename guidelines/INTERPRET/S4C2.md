# S4C2: Leaderboard Management

## Brief
The harness systematically compares multiple systems through rigorous statistical frameworks, supporting robust ranking and multi-objective comparison.

## Essential Features

1. **Multi-System Ranking**: Rank multiple systems based on performance metrics with support for complex scoring schemes
2. **Statistical Ranking Methods**: Apply rigorous statistical frameworks for ranking (confidence intervals, significance-aware ranking)
3. **Ranking Uncertainty Quantification**: Compute and display uncertainty in rankings (confidence intervals, bootstrapped ranks)
4. **Multi-Objective Comparison**: Support comparison across multiple metrics simultaneously with Pareto frontier analysis
5. **Leaderboard Persistence & Updates**: Maintain persistent leaderboard state and support incremental updates with new systems
