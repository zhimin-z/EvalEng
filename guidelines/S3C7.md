# S3C7: Fairness Assessment

## Brief
The harness evaluates whether performance metrics are consistent across demographic groups and subpopulations, identifying measurement disparities.

## Essential Features

1. **Subgroup Performance Computation**: Compute performance metrics separately for demographic groups and subpopulations (including rater demographics when applicable)
2. **Fairness Criteria Testing**: Test multiple fairness definitions (demographic parity, equalized odds, predictive parity) to assess equity across groups
3. **Intersectional Analysis**: Analyze fairness across combinations of protected attributes (e.g., race × gender) to detect complex disparities
4. **Statistical Significance Testing**: Conduct statistical tests to identify significant performance disparities between subgroups
5. **Bias Detection & Reporting**: Identify, quantify, and report fairness violations and measurement disparities with actionable insights
