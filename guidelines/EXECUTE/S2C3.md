# S2C3: Contamination Detection

## Brief
The harness identifies and reports evidence of data leakage between training and evaluation data, flagging potential validity threats.

## Essential Features

1. **Training Data Comparison**: Compare evaluation instances against known training corpora to detect potential contamination
2. **Exact Match Detection**: Identify exact duplicates between training and evaluation sets (full instance matching)
3. **Near-Duplicate Detection**: Detect near-duplicates using similarity metrics (n-gram overlap, embedding similarity, edit distance)
4. **Leakage Evidence Reporting**: Generate detailed reports of detected contamination with specific evidence and matched instances
5. **Contamination Severity Assessment**: Quantify the extent and impact of data leakage (percentage contaminated, severity levels)
