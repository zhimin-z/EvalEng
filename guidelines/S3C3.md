# S3C3: Output Result Validation

## Brief
The harness applies sanity checks to evaluation results, flagging implausible patterns that suggest implementation errors, data quality issues, or validity threats.

## Essential Features

1. **Result Range Validation**: Verify that scores fall within expected/valid ranges for the measurement protocol
2. **Distribution Sanity Checks**: Flag suspicious score distributions (all zeros, all perfect scores, unusual clustering)
3. **Outlier Detection**: Identify implausible outliers that may indicate scoring errors or data quality issues
4. **Cross-Instance Consistency Checking**: Detect suspicious patterns across instances (identical scores, monotonic patterns)
5. **Anomaly Reporting & Diagnostics**: Report detected anomalies with diagnostic information to aid investigation
